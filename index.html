Your Investment FAQs

How can I an open an investment account?

After you get in touch with us, we'll ask you for the necessary information required to open your account with one of our trusted brokers; then we enter into an agreement, then we open your account. Once your account is up and running, you transfer the capital money to that account. The account can be managed by yourself, only. And the capital can be transfered out only to the same source where it came from, which secures your initial capital.

Why do you open an account on my behalf?

Of course you can open the account all by yourself but then you will not benefit from our trading services to manage and grow your investment. 

Why Brown Armadillo and not someone else?

Key differences:
* No fees upon depositing your initial capital
* No maintenance monthly nor annual fees
* Access and withdraw your money anytime. We only ask that you to let us know in advance by 24h so we stop any market operation that is in progress.

How much will it cost me?

We do not charge commissions on capital, we only charge a flat fee on the profits we make for you. Please check our simulator to have an idea.

How can my investment help in financing my real estate loan?

Financing your real estate can be expensive. By placing a smaller amount with Brown Armadillo to be managed on your behalf, you increase your capital and in return you will be able to finance your dream home or real estate investment quicker and smarter.
